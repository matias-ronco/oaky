Oaky Gold-Silver

BANG BANG shoot shoot BIG MESS adventure!

Some very interesting facts about this baby cowboy:

  • Oaky was created in 1956 by Manuel García Ferré, as Mr. Gold-Silver's only son as one of the main characters in the TV show 'Hijitus'.
  • Oaky lives in Trulalá and his dad is the richest and most powerful man in town, but in spite of his wealth, Oaky prefers to settle his arrangements aided only by his two big loussy guns.
  • His friend Hijitus is kinda' homeless. Oaky helps him in his adventures but has never shown no will to provide some decent housing to help his friend out of the streets.
  • Ocassionally, Oaky also cooperates with Professor Neurus evil gang, demonstrating that rich people have a crooked sense of morale, even when still wearing diapers.
  • His best friend is called Larguirucho, who's a dumb but friendly thug most likely addicted to low-end drugs, although this has never been clarified on the TV show to avoid censorship issues.
  • He's deeply in love with his opposite-house neighbour, who ignores him with indolence and to whom he has composed a song claiming:

    'My opposite house little neighbour
    Has driven me insane
    She says to me 'no way!'
    and hits me in the head.
    At nightime I dream of her
    At daytime I write poetry
    Because of her I swear
    I've lost my appetite.'
